***To integrate with Zoho Marketing Hub follow, the below steps.***


## Process of Authorization and Integration
1. At First, Open your Bit Integrations Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **“Create Integrations”** button.
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/Create Integration" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />


2. Now select Fluent Forms that you want to integrate with Zoho Marketing Hub.
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/select contact form" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />

3. Select the form name from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with Zoho Marketing Hub. And click on the **“Next” button**. And select **Zoho Marketing Hub.**
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/fluent-form" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/select marketing hub" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />

4. Integration Name: Set the proper integration name Default it's taken “Zoho Marketing Hub”.
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/integration name" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />

5. Select a data center from the dropdown. Then click on the *Zoho API Console*. Navigate to **API Console-**> **Get Started**-> **Server-based Applications.** 
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/select data center" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/get-started" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/server-based-applications" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />


6. Fill those fields (Homepage URL and Authorized Redirect URLs) the same as your Integration Homepage. Then click **CREATE** button
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/create api" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />


7. A Client ID and a Client Secret will be generated. Then go to the Client Secret tab
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/client id and secret" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="300" />

8. Then copy the Client ID and Client Secret and paste it to the Bit Integrations integration page. Then click on the **Authorize** button (wait for a few seconds). After the successful authorization click on the **Next** button
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/authorize" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="300" />

9. Select the list for the lead collection from the drop-down. Before that make sure you have already created a list on your marketing hub account.
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/select list" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="300" />


10.	When you fill-up the contact list field, you will find the Map Field option. You have to map all the necessary form fields with the related Zoho Marketing Hub.
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/field mapping" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="300" />



11.	 After clicking on Finish & Save, the Mailchimp integration is ready.
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/finish and save" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />

12. We have an option in every integration "Timeline," where you can check integration success or error status.
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/integration dashbord" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="Zoho Marketing Hub/timeline" alt="Zoho Marketing Hub" w="1020" h="350" />
