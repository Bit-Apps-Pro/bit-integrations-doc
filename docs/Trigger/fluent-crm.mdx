> **Note: Fluent CRM is available on the free and pro version.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **Create Integration** button.

   <Image src="trigger/_create-integrations" alt="Fluent CRM" w="1020" h="350" />

2. Now select the **Fluent CRM** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/fluent-crm/_select-trigger" alt="Fluent CRM" w="1020" h="350" />

3. Select the Task( `A tag is added to contact`, `A tag is removed from contact`, `A contact is added to a list`, `A contact is remove from a list`, `A contact set to a specific status`, `A contact is create`) from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**. And click on the **Next** button.

   <Image src="trigger/fluent-crm/select-form-or-task-name" alt="Fluent CRM" w="1020" h="350"/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action. To know more about authorization, Go to **Actions** from the left side menu and select the Action you want to integrate with your chosen trigger.
:::
