> **Note: JetFormBuilder is available on the free and pro version.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **_“Create Integration”_** button.

   <Image src="trigger/_create-integrations" alt="JetFormBuilder" w="1020" h="350" />

2. Now select the **JetFormBuilder** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/jetformbuilder/select-trigger" alt="JetFormBuilder" w="1020" h="350" />

3.	Bit integrations will automatically generate a **webhook URL** & you have to copy that link. And click on the **`fetch`** button.   
   * Now navigate to **JetFormBuilder** 
   * `Edit Form` (The form that you will integrate) 
   * Setting (Jetform tab) 
   * go to post submission action 
   * Create a new `action` 
   * select call webhook (from the drop-down list) 
   * click on `edit` action icon. Paste the copied Webhook URL into the URL field and click the `save` button. 
   * Submit something to your Jet form. 
   <Video src="jetformbuilder-fetch-data" alt="JetFormBuilder" w="1020" h="350" />
4. Then go to the Bit integration page and click on the set action button.
   <Image src="trigger/jetformbuilder/click-set-action" alt="JetFormBuilder" w="1020" h="350" />


<br/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action. To know more about authorization, Go to **Actions** from the left side menu and select the Action you want to integrate with your chosen trigger.
:::
