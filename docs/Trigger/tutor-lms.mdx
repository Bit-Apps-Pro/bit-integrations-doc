> **Note: Tutor LMS is available on the free and pro version.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **_“Create Integration”_** button.

   <Image src="trigger/_create-integrations" alt="Tutor LMS" w="1020" h="350" />

2. Now select the **Tutor LMS** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/tutor-lms/select-trigger" alt="Tutor LMS" w="1020" h="350" />

3.	Select the Task (`Course-Enroll`, `User attempts(submit) a quiz`, `Completed a lesson`, `Complete a course`, `User achieves a targeted percentage on a quiz`) from the Drop-Down list,   
And Select the Course from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**. Then click on the **Next** button.

   <Image src="trigger/tutor-lms/select-form-or-task-name" alt="Tutor LMS" w="1020" h="350"/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action. To know more about authorization, Go to **Actions** from the left side menu and select the Action you want to integrate with your chosen trigger.
:::
