> **Note: Woocommerce is available on the free and pro version.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **_“Create Integration”_** button.

   <Image src="trigger/woocommerce/create-integrations" alt="Woocommerce" w="1020" h="350" />

2. Now select the **Woocommerce** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/woocommerce/select-trigger" alt="Woocommerce" w="1020" h="350" />

3. Select the Task **( `Customer-Create` `Customer-Edit`, `Customer-Delete`, `Product-Create`, `Product-Edit`, `Product-Delete`, `Order-Create`, `Order-Edit`, O`rder-Delete` )** from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**. And click on the **“Next”** button.

   <Image src="trigger/woocommerce/selec-form-or-task-name" alt="Woocommerce" w="1020" h="350"/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action. To know more about authorization, Go to **Actions** from the left side menu and select the Action you want to integrate with your chosen trigger.
:::
