> **Note: Bit Form is available in the free and pro versions.**

***To integrate with Bit Form follow, the below steps.***

## Process of Authorization and Integration

1. Search & Select Bit Form from the actions pages.
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/select-bit-form" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />


2. Integration Name: Set the proper integration name. Default it's taken “Bit Form”.
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/integration-name" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />


3. **Your Domain Name & Client id:**  To get Your Domain Name & Client id, open your Bit Form Dashboard and navigate to App Setting ->API. You have to copy the Domain URL & API Key and paste them into the integration setting. And Click on the authorize button then click on the Next button. 

 <Image src="actions/bit-form/domain-url-client-id" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/get-domail-url-api-key" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />


4. After successful authorization. Select the Form from the drop-down option. You have to create a Form before on your Bit Form. 
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/select-bit-form" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />

5. You will find the Map Field option and map the corresponding form field. After mapping the desired field, click on Next
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/select-bit-form" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />

6. Now save the integration by clicking the "Finish & Save." Button
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/finish-and-save" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />

7. Bit Integrations have an option in every integration **`Timeline`** where you can check integration success or error status.
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/timeline" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/bit-form/sucess-or-error-status" alt="Bit Form" w="1020" h="350" />

9. Also, you can **`Edit`** **`Clone`** **`Delete`** the integration.

 <Image src="actions/acumbamail/edit-clone-delete" alt="Elastic Email" w="1020" h="350" />
