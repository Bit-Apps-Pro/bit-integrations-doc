> **Note: Slack is available on the free and pro version.**

***To integrate with Slack follow, the below steps.***


## Process of Authorization and Integration

1.	Search & Select **Slack** from the actions pages
 <Image src="actions/slack/search-and-select" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

2. **Integration Name:** Set the proper integration name. Default it's taken 'Slack'.
 <Image src="actions/slack/default-integration-name" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

3.	After that, you have to copy the API key from your Slack account & paste the key on the field. To get the API key, click on **Slack API Console** or go to [api.slack.com/apps](https://api.slack.com/apps)  
    **Setup Proccess**
    1. click on `Create New App`/Create an App: `From scratch`.
    2.	Type your app name
    3.	Select your workspace from the Drop-Down list that you want to send the message. and click on `Create App` button.
    4.	In the left sidebar menu, navigate to `OAuth & Permissions` tab under Features.
    5.	Scroll Down and go to User Token Scopes under **scopes** section and add scopes `chat:write`, `channels:read`, `channels:write`, `files:read`, `files:write`.
    6.	Go to OAuth Tokens for Your Workspace section and click on `install to workspace` button.
 <Video src="slack-access-token" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

4. Now copy the API key and paste it into the integrations dashboard. And Click on **`“Authorize”`** button.

 <Image src="actions/slack/authorize" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />


5. Click on **`Next`** to set up the other integration settings ahead.

6. After completing the authorization process, Select the Channels List from the drop-down option. You have to create a list before on your Slack account.
 <Image src="actions/slack/slack-channels-list" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

7. You can design your message in two ways `HTML` `Markdown v2`.

### HTML
1. You can put dynamic field names email body.
2. You can copy and paste previous design template another editor.
3. You can edit styling by the help of rich text editor.
 <Image src="actions/slack/html-message-design" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

### Markdown v2
1. You can put dynamic field names email body.
2. You can edit messages as wish.
 <Image src="actions/slack/markdown-message-design" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

## Actions
1. You can select the action Attachment right below the Message’ field. Enabling this action, you can send – `Audio`, `Video`, `Photo`, or `Document` from Trigger to slack. After this, click on the `Next` option.

 <Image src="actions/slack/enable-attachment" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

8. Now save the integration by clicking the **`Finish & Save`** Button

 <Image src="actions/slack/finish-save" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

8. We have an option in every integration **`Timeline`** where you can check integration success or error status.
 <Image src="actions/slack/slack-timeline" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/slack/slack-log" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />

9. Also, you can **`Edit`** **`Clone`** **`Delete`** the integration.

 <Image src="actions/slack/slack-option" alt="Slack" w="1020" h="350" />
