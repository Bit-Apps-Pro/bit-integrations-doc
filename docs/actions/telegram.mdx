***To integrate with Telegram follow, the below steps.***


## Process of Authorization and Integration
> **Note: Telegram is only available on the pro version.**

1.	Search & Select **Telegram** from the actions pages
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_select-telegram" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />
 
4.	Integration Name: Set the proper integration name Default it's taken “Telegram”.
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_integration-name" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />

5. To get the *API Key*, visit your telegram account. Now, search for **@BotFather.** If you have created a bot before, then just copy the **API key** & past the key on the API Key field on the Trigger integration page else, create a new one by sending a command **/newbot** to **@BotFather**
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_bot-api" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_paste-bot-api-key" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />


6.	**Authorize** the entire settings.
7.	Click on Next for further integration settings ahead.
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_authorize" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />

 
8.	Click on the drop-down & select the specific chat list you have created earlier on Telegram.
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_select-chat-list" alt="Encharge" w="1020" h="300" />

 A. Sometimes you may not find the lists of your telegram account. One thing you can do that – go to your telegram account, then remove the administrator’s bot from that specific list or group & add again the administrator’s bot.
    
 B. To remove the bot & re-add the bot follow the path: **Click on your desired list/group/Channel => Go to settings of that list/group/Channel => Administrator => Remove the promoted bot & add again the administrator’s bot**


9.	Select the parse_mode method to send text notifications to the specified group or channel. You can use either **HTML** or **Markdown** format to send a message.
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_create-massage" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />

10. Now, type a message as your preference. You can select Form Fields to fetch the user information if you want. Also, you can mention any group or channels from this message field.
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_create-massage2" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />

## Actions
1.	You can select the action Attachment right below the Message’ field. Enabling this action, you can send – Audio, Video, Photo, or Document from Trigger to Telegram. After this, click on the Next option.
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_attachment-actions" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />

## Save Integration
1.	To successfully integrate your form with Telegram click the **Finish & Save** button.
 <Image src="actions/telegram/_finish-and-save" alt="Telegram" w="1020" h="350" />
