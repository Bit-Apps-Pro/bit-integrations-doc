> **Note: Twilio is only available on the pro version.**

***To integrate with Twilio follow, the below steps.***


## Process of Authorization and Integration

1.	Search & Select **Twilio** from the actions pages
 <Image src="actions/twilio/select-action-twilio" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />

2. **Integration Name:** Set the proper integration name. Default it's taken **Twilio**.
 <Image src="actions/twilio/integration-name" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />

3.	After that, you have to copy the *Account SID*, *Auth Token*, and *From (Phone Number)* from your Twilio account & paste on the field.   
**To get the Account SID, Auth Token, and from (Phone Number),** 
-	click on `Twilio Console` or go to **Twilio console Dashboard**
-	click on *Account* => *API Keys & Tokens*
-	Now copy the *Account SID*, *Auth Token* and paste it into the integration’s dashboard. 
-	Enter your twilio phone number *From (Phone Number)* field
-	Click on **“Authorize”** button.

 <Image src="actions/twilio/click-twilio-console" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/twilio/create-api" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/twilio/authorize" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />

:::info
From (Phone Number) means Sender Phone Number
:::

4. Click on **`Next`** to set up the other integration settings ahead.

5. After completing the authorization process, you will find the Map Field option. You have to map **Select Number** fields with the related Twilio field.  
 <Image src="actions/twilio/twilio-field-map" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />

6. You can type your message and also can put dynamic fields (Form Fields or Task Fields).
 <Image src="actions/twilio/twilio-message-field-map" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />

7. Now, click on the `Next` button to move forward for further settings. 

:::info
`Select Number` means Receiver Phone Number
:::

6. Now save the integration by clicking the **`Finish & Save`** Button

 <Image src="actions/twilio/finish-save" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />

:::info
If you use Twilio trial account then you must verify the recipient number.   
To verify the recipient number click on the link: [Click here](https://console.twilio.com/us1/develop/phone-numbers/manage/verified)
:::

7. We have an option in every integration **`Timeline`** where you can check integration success or error status.
 <Image src="actions/twilio/twilio-timeline" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/twilio/twilio-log" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />

8. Also, you can **`Edit`** **`Clone`** **`Delete`** the integration.

 <Image src="actions/twilio/twilio-option" alt="Twilio" w="1020" h="350" />
