> **Note: WP Courseware is available in the free and pro versions.**

***To integrate with WP Courseware follow, the below steps.***


## Process of Authorization and Integration

1. Search & Select **WP Courseware** from the actions pages
 <Image src="actions/wp-courseware/search-and-select" alt="WP Courseware" w="1020" h="350" />
 
2. **Integration Name:** Set the proper integration name. Default it's taken “WP Courseware ”.
 <Image src="actions/wp-courseware/integration-name" alt="WP Courseware" w="1020" h="350" />

3.	Now click on the **Connect** Button to connect with WP Courseware; after successfully connect, click on the **Next** button.
 <Image src="actions/wp-courseware/authorize" alt="WP Courseware" w="1020" h="350" />
 <Image src="actions/wp-courseware/next-button" alt="WP Courseware" w="1020" h="350" />



4. Select the Action (`Enroll the user in a course`, `Unenroll user from a course`) from the Drop-Down list, And Select the Course/ Lessons from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with WP Courseware . Then click on the **Next** button.
 <Image src="actions/wp-courseware/select-action-and-course" alt="WP Courseware" w="1020" h="350" />

5. Now save the integration by clicking the "**Finish & Save.**" Button
 <Image src="actions/wp-courseware/finis-and-save" alt="WP Courseware" w="1020" h="350" />
