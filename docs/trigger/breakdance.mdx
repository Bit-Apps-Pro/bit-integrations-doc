> **Note: Breakdance is available on the free and pro version.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **_“Create Integration”_** button.

   <Image src="trigger/_create-integrations" alt="Breakdance" w="1020" h="350" />

2. Now search and select the **Breakdance** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/breakdance/select-trigger" alt="Breakdance" w="1020" h="350" />

3. Select the form from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**. And click on the **“Next”** button.

   <Image src="trigger/breakdance/select-form-or-task-name" alt="Breakdance" w="1020" h="350"/>

:::note   
**Note: You must select `Bit Integrations pro` as the form submission action from the Breakdance Form Builder settings.**
:::
   <Image src="trigger/breakdance/select-custom-action" alt="Breakdance" w="1020" h="350"/>

<br/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action. To know more about authorization, Go to **Actions** from the left side menu and select the Action you want to integrate with your chosen trigger.
:::
