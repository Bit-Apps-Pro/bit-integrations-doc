> **Note:  Bit Assist is available in the free and pro versions.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **_“Create Integration”_** button.

   <Image src="trigger/_create-integrations" alt="Bit Assist"  w="1020" h="350"/>

2. Now select the **Bit Assist** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/bit-assist/search-and-select" alt="Bit Assist" w="1020" h="350"/>

3.	Bit integrations will automatically generate a **webhook URL** & you have to copy that link. And click on the **`fetch`** button. 
 - Now navigate to the Bit Assist dashboard 
 - edit your widget 
 - edit your custom form
 - scroll down and Paste the copied Webhook URL into the URL field
 - Update the Channel
 - After completing these steps submit something to your Bit Assist custom form. Or please follow the below video here is a demo video to Fetch Bit Assist's data.
<Video src="bit-assist-fetch" alt="Bit Assist" w="1020" h="350" />
  

4. After successfully Fetched the data then click on Set Action button
   <Image src="trigger/bit-assist/click-set-action-button" alt="Bit Assist" w="1020" h="350"/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action. To know more about authorization, Go to **Actions** from the left side menu and select the Action you want to integrate with your chosen trigger.
:::
