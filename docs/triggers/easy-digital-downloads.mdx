> **Note: Easy Digital Downloads is available on the free and pro version.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **_“Create Integration”_** button.

   <Image src="trigger/_create-integrations" alt="Easy Digital Downloads" w="1020" h="350" />

2. Now select the **Easy Digital Downloads** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/easy-digital-downloads/select-trigger" alt="Easy Digital Downloads" w="1020" h="350" />

3. Select the form from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**. And click on the **“Next”** button.

   <Image src="trigger/easy-digital-downloads/select-form-or-task-name" alt="Easy Digital Downloads" w="1020" h="350"/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action.
