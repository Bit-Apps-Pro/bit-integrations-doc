> **Note: Formidable Forms is available in the free and pro versions.**

1. At First, Open your Bit Integration Dashboard and create an integrations by clicking the **“Create Integration”** button.

   <Image src="trigger/formidable-forms/_create-integration" alt="formidable-forms"  w="1020" h="350"/>

2. Now select the **Formidable Forms** that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**.

   <Image src="trigger/formidable-forms/_formidable-forms" alt="formidable-forms" w="1020" h="350"/>

3. Select the form from the Drop-Down list that you want to integrate with Bit Integrations **Actions**. And click on the **“Next”** button.

   <Image src="trigger/formidable-forms/_select-form" alt="formidable-forms" w="1020" h="350"/>

:::info
  After completing these steps, the **Actions** list will appear, and select the action you want to integrate with your selected **Trigger**. Now you have to **authorize** the Action. To know more about authorization, Go to **Actions** from the left side menu and select the Action you want to integrate with your chosen trigger.
:::
